\begin{frame}{Example 4}
Find all zeros for $f(x) = 12x^5 - 20x^4 + 19x^3 - 6x^2 - 2x + 1$   \newline\\  \pause
Potential Rational Zeros:
\[ \pm 1, \, \pm \frac{1}{2}, \, \pm \frac{1}{3}, \, \pm \frac{1}{4}, \, \pm \frac{1}{6}, \, \pm \frac{1}{12},\]
